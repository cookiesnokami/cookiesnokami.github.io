Welcome to my personal page

I'm a Ph. D. student in Literary Studies in Complutense University of Madrid (UCM). I research frames of the fantastic, through semiotics, between Murakami Haruki’s novels and Persona 2 videogames by Atlus. The main objective of my dissertation is to discover reasons behind the similitudes of the fantastic phenomena in two different media expression. Furthermore, I have other research interests: Japanese literature, theory of the fantastic, videogames, and Japanese culture and its reception.

Before I enrolled to this doctorate program, I’ve graduated in degrees. First one is a bachelor’s degree in Librarian and Information Sciences in (UCM). Then, I started a Master of Arts in East Asian Studies with a specialization in Japanese Studies in Autonomous University of Madrid. After my graduation, I returned to UCM and completed a Master of Arts in Literature with a master dissertation about theories of the fantastic.

Besides my academic career, I worked in several companies related with IT. There, I developed my skills in management and organization of knowledge, digital media, translation, and communication. Also, I participated in several companies related, from business that developed military equipment to public works enterprises.

I am intellectually inquisitive person. Thus, I enjoy a good book, comic or an interesting discussion with my friends. Additionally, I usually play video games and tabletop RPGs. Some of my favorite video games are: Amnesia: The Dark Descent, La-Mulana, Master of Orion II: Battle at Antares, Phantasy Star IV, Planescape: Torment, Shadowrun, Shin Megami Tensei, Simon the Sorcerer, and The Legends of Heroes.
