Welcome to my personal page

I'm a Ph. D. student in Literary Studies at Complutense University of Madrid (UCM). I research frames of the fantastic, through semiotics, between Murakami Haruki’s novels and Persona 2 video games by Atlus. The main objective of my dissertation is to discover the reasons behind the similitudes of the fantastic phenomena in two different media expression. Furthermore, I have other research interests: Japanese literature, theory of the fantastic, video games, and Japanese culture and its reception.

Before I enrolled in this doctorate program, I have graduated in several degrees. First one is a bachelor’s degree in Librarian and Information Sciences in UCM. Then, I started a Master of Arts in East Asian Studies with a specialization in Japanese Studies at Autonomous University of Madrid (UAM). After my graduation, I returned to UCM and completed a Master of Arts in Literature with a master dissertation about theories of the fantastic.

Besides my academic career, I worked in different companies related to IT. There, I developed my skills in management and organization of knowledge, digital media, translation, and communication. Also, I participated in several projects related fields like military equipment or public works enterprises.

I am an intellectual and inquisitive person. Thus, I enjoy a good book, comic or an interesting discussion with my friends. Additionally, I usually play video games and tabletop RPGs. Some of my favourite video games are: Amnesia: The Dark Descent, La-Mulana, Master of Orion II: Battle at Antares, Phantasy Star IV, Planescape: Torment, Shadowrun, Shin Megami Tensei, Simon the Sorcerer, and The Legends of Heroes.
